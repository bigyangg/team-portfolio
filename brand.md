## Tone and voice

Use direct and factual language. Prioritize clarity, measurable outcomes, and concise statements.

Avoid hype language and exaggerated claims. Keep copy formal, human, and policy-safe.

Voice example:

> 27 experts mapped across AI, chemistry, and systems engineering, with CV status and source traceability.

## Usage dos and don'ts

**Do**

- Use existing CSS variables in `globals.css` instead of hardcoded colors.
- Keep profile cards high-contrast and short-form.
- Use `font-mono` + tabular numbers for metric values.

**Don't**

- Introduce bright playful accents unrelated to government context.
- Mix multiple competing gradients in one view.
- Reintroduce serif headings for this brand direction.
